> “I guess the reason I started my cosmetic company is that I wasn’t patient enough to be a philosopher, and I wasn’t tolerant enough to be an architect.”

An exceptionally cultured man and a deeply sensitive soul, he was shaped by the enduring legacy of the European Grand Tour. Born and raised within Melbourne’s Greek community in the conservative 1960s, he carried that dual influence into a singular vision, one that would go on to create one of the world’s most desired, elegant, and sophisticated cosmetic brand.
