> “Sono sempre stato più interessato all'imperfezione, poiché è quella che spicca nella massa.”
>
> “I have always been more interested in imperfection, as it is what stands out from the crowd.”

Renzo created an ecosystem in his own image. Brave, irreverent, fun, positive, but also deeply ironic and ready to turn the world of prejudice upside down. His personality influenced the whole company and attracted talented yet unconventional managers and creatives from all over the world to the tiny Venetian town of Molvena, making it more international than Oxford Circus in London. In this caleidoscope of minds, lateral thinking flourishes.
